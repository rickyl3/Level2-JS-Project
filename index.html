JS Level 2 Project - Match Game

Instructions:

Using your memory, try to match every image. Click on each image to reveal them but you can only reveal two at a time. If the two images that are revealed are not the same image, their images will return back to a question mark after 1 second. If the two images are the same image, they will stay revealed since you were able to match them. Try to match every image in the shortest amount of clicks as you can! Your score will be recorded for you below the grid.
